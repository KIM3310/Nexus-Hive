# Monetization Playbook - Nexus-Hive

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2B governed analytics
- **Primary buyer:** Data platform teams, BI owners, and operations leaders who need self-service questions without uncontrolled SQL.
- **Revenue wedge:** Governed NL-to-SQL with policy checks, audit trail, and warehouse adapter path.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Governed analytics cockpit pilot** - pricing hypothesis: $5k-$12k discovery + demo
2. **Warehouse adapter setup** - pricing hypothesis: $15k-$40k implementation
3. **Policy template and audit-readiness pack** - pricing hypothesis: $2k-$8k/month governance support

## Acquisition Channels

- BI governance content
- Snowflake/Databricks migration conversations
- Recorded local SQLite demo walkthrough

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- make verify passes
- Seeded question-to-SQL trace is inspectable
- Policy rejection examples are visible

## Risk Boundaries

- Do not run write queries by default
- Live warehouses require scoped credentials
- Human review required for high-risk metrics

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Publish 5 governed-query examples
- **60 days:** Build data-leader one-pager
- **90 days:** Sell one adapter pilot

## Metrics That Matter

- Policy pass/reject clarity
- Analyst time saved
- Audited query coverage

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
